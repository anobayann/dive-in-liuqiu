Xiao Liuqiu island
小琉球
One of the best diving spot in Taiwan

Xiaoliuqiu island, also known as Liuqiu, Ryukyu, Lamay or Lambai island is surrundend by hundreads of green sea turtles! This tiny island is 6.8 km2 which made it one the best place on the world to see them. The water is very clear, visibility 15 to 30 meters and the water temperature is around 24 to 28°C depending the season. Little Liuqiu is enjoyable all year as it is not affected by typhoons and northeast moonsoons. This duty free island is easily accessible from Kaoshung and perfect for a a few days of vitamin sea
