Xiao Liuqiu island
小琉球
One of the best places to dive in Taiwan

Would you like to scuba dive with sea turtles?

Xiao Liuqiu island, also known as Liuqiu, Ryukyu, Lamay or Lambai island is surrounded by hundreds of green sea turtles! This tiny island is 6.8 km2 which made it one the best places in the world to see them (almost 100% chance :D).
The water is very clear and warm, visibility 15 to 30 meters and the water temperature is around 25 to 28°C depending on the season. Little Liuqiu is enjoyable all year as it is not affected by typhoons and northeast monsoons. It is easily accessible from Kaoshiung and perfect for a few days of vitamins SEA!
